About Us
MentAR is an AR-based educational app for school students, which helps them visualize key concepts and immerse themselves in the world of fun learning. MentAR is made for classes 7th & 8th and makes studying engaging with interactive classes and quizzes.

Powered by Augmented Reality, MentAR provides a visually-rich learning experience at your fingertips, so you can learn anytime and anywhere. Using MentAR, you can perform various lab experiments at any time in the comfort and safety of your home.

MentAR is developed by AugnaAR, a Bits Pilani startup and among the top start-ups of India, recognized by many reputational institutes and companies in India.
